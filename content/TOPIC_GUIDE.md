# JLM Wines: Sensory Guide & Metaphor Framework

This document serves as the master guide for explaining wine characteristics to JLM Wines customers. We use a unified **Audio (Music)** and **Visual (Art)** framework to make abstract concepts tangible.

The core philosophy: **The Flavor (Fruit)** is the *Subject* or *Song*. The characteristics below describe *how* that subject is presented.

### 4. VISUALIZING THE CONCEPTS (Video & Media)

For video content (Instagram/TikTok), use a **Split-Screen Progression**:

*   **Left Screen (Audio):** An audio mixer/EQ graphic sliding from Low to High.
*   **Right Screen (Visual):** An image transforming from Sketch $\to$ Painting $\to$ Mosaic, or Pastel $\to$ Vivid.
*   **Center:** The wine pour, visually matching the intensity (e.g., a light pour vs. a heavy, rich glug).
