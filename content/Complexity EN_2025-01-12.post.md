## TITLE
How Much is Happening in Your Glass?

## EXCERPT
How much is happening in your wine? Complexity measures how many flavors are playing at once, so you can pick the right level for your mood.

<p style="font-size:1.15em">Have you ever noticed that some wines showcase one or two flavors while others seem to unfold into something new with every sip? <strong>Complexity</strong> describes how many different flavors are dancing on your tastebuds at once.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:columns -->
<div class="wp-block-columns"><!-- wp:column {"width":"55%"} -->
<div class="wp-block-column" style="flex-basis:55%"><!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">The Spectrum of Complexity</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Lower Complexity:</strong> These wines have one clear flavor — often fresh fruit like blueberries or crisp green apples. They're refreshing, easy-drinking, and perfect when you want something nice without overthinking it.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Medium Complexity:</strong> You'll taste the main fruit, but also notice something secondary — a floral note, a hint of spice, or a touch of earthiness. More to discover in every sip, but still easy to decipher.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Higher Complexity:</strong> A multi-layered experience. Deep fruit that evolves, plus hints of oak, toasted nuts, vanilla, herbs, or earthy undertones. The sensations shift from the moment the wine hits your tongue to the moment you swallow. Every sip reveals something new.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Finding Your Level</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Complexity isn't about quality — it's about the experience you're in the mood for.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>A simple wine is great for a casual afternoon or a weeknight dinner. A highly complex wine is for those times you want to slow down and notice how the flavors unfold — maybe with a special meal or when the wine itself is the focus.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":67090,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none","className":"mobile-only"} -->

<h3 class="wp-block-heading">A Way to Picture It</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Think of it like music — same song, same volume, but change who's playing:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>A solo or duet</strong> — One or two instruments. The melody is clear, direct, easy to follow.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>A small ensemble</strong> — A few more instruments join. You still hear the main melody, but now there's harmony.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>A full orchestra</strong> — Layers of sound. The melody is woven into something richer. Every listen reveals something new.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list --></div>
<!-- /wp:group -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">The Complexity Scale</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Each wine on our site shows a <strong>Complexity rating from 1 to 5</strong>:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Levels 1 &amp; 2:</strong> One clear flavor, clean and direct.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Level 3:</strong> Main flavor plus harmonies to discover.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Levels 4 &amp; 5:</strong> Layers that unfold and evolve.</li>
