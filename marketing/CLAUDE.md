# Marketing Folder

Campaigns, promotions, and customer communications for JLM Wines.

## Operational Procedures

- **Email HTML (promo / companion emails)** → `marketing/EMAIL_GUIDELINES.md` — design decisions, Mailchimp process, Hebrew checklist, and what not to do. Reference HTML: `marketing/newsletter/issues/2026-06-handling-en.html` + HE sibling.
- **Print newsletter (monthly insert)** → `marketing/NEWSLETTER_PLAN.md` — full workflow: layout, Making Wine series, Drive template/docx delivery, issue cadence. Per-issue files in `marketing/newsletter/issues/`.

## Related Folders

- `business/` — Strategy docs including `CONTENT_STRATEGY.md`
- `content/` — Blog posts, guides, educational content
- `website/` — Frontend/website code

## Brand Voice Quick Reference

All marketing must reflect:

- **Friendly, personal, never talks down**
- **Anti-snob** — wine without pretension
- **Customer:** People who drink wine (not "wine lovers"), want convenience and consistency
- **Owner's tone:** Polite, earnest, upbeat, service-dedicated, never negative about competitors

Full guidelines: `business/CONTENT_STRATEGY.md`
